# FNP-Sales-Performance-Analysis-Excel
### Project Overview: 
Dynamic Excel dashboard analyzing sales revenue, customer spending, product performance, city-wise orders, and occasion-based trends. Enables business teams to track KPIs, identify revenue drives, and make informed sales and marketing decisions.

### Objectives
- Analyze monthly revenue trends
- Identify top-performing products and cities
- Track revenue by category and occasions
- Monitor delivery performance and customer spend

### Key Metrics
- Total Orders Placed: 1000
- Total Revenue: ₹35,20,984
- Average Delivery Time: 5.53 days
- Average Customer Spend: ₹3,520.98

### Dashboard Insights
- Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generate highest revenue
- Magman Quia Gift Set is the top-selling product
- Imphal and Dhanbad are top cities by orders
- Seasonal spikes in monthly revenue observed

- Slicers & Filters
- Dashboard Design
